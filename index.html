0. System Overview & MVP Scope

The Modern Market Information System (MMIS) HUB is a comprehensive digital ecosystem designed to modernize traditional market operations. It bridges the gap between vendors, suppliers, and market administrators through real-time data, secure financial processing, and streamlined logistics management.

MVP Implementation Scope

The current MVP (Minimum Viable Product) focuses on the core lifecycle of market participants:

  • Multi-role Authentication & Authorization
  • Entity Onboarding & Approval Workflows
  • Vendor Inventory & Shop Management
  • Gate Access Control & Supplier Tracking
  • Basic Financial Reporting & Revenue Oversight
  • Integrated Support Ticket System

Core Modules

Auth & Identity

Secure login and role-based access control.

Onboarding

Step-by-step registration for all entities.

Market Registry

Centralized database of registered entities.

Vendor Module

Inventory management and shop configuration.

Gate Management

Entry/Exit tracking with token generation.

Financials

Revenue tracking and tax liability monitoring.

Support

Maintenance and infrastructure ticket management.

5. Gate Staff Operations Walkthrough

The narrator logs in as Gate Staff to demonstrate market entry and exit procedures.

Step 1: Generate Access Token (Registered Supplier)

  • Action: Navigate to the Gate Terminal.
  • Action: Enter the Supplier's Email or ID code (e.g., # azwafahadsan@gmail.com).
  • Action: Enter the Vehicle Plate and Vehicle Type.
  • Action: Click Generate Access Token.
  • Result: A token is generated and displayed (e.g., KAB-9EA070).

Step 2: Generate Access Token (Guest Supplier)

  • Action: Switch to the Guest tab.
  • Action: Enter the Guest's Full Name and ID Number.
  • Action: Enter Vehicle details and click Generate Access Token.

Step 3: Record Sales & Exit

  • Action: Enter the generated token code into the Search bar.
  • Action: Click Record Sales.
  • Action: Select the good type (e.g., Clothes or Vegetables) and enter the Total Sale Value (e.g., $100 or $300).
  • Action: Click Submit Dispatch Record.
  • Action: Click Finalize Exit to log the supplier's departure from the market.

9. Technical Support & Troubleshooting

Common Issues

  • Q: Login fails after registration?
  • A: Ensure your Market Admin has approved your account.
  • Q: Token not recognized at gate?
  • A: Check for typos or ensure the token hasn't expired.
